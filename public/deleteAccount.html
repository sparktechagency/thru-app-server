How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Profile tab.
  2. Scroll down and tap Delete Account.
    You’ll see a confirmation pop-up asking for your password.
  3. In the pop-up, enter your Password to confirm and tap Delete.
  4. Your account will be deleted and you will be signed out automatically.
    This action is permanent and can’t be undone.
